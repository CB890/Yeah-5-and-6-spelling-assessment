üöÄ Getting Started

  1. 1Enter your name (optional): Type your name in the box so your teacher can see your results.

üìñ How the Quiz Works

Reading the Story

You'll see a story with some words missing. The computer will read the whole story to you first, including the missing words.

üîä Listen: Plays just one word

üéØ Second Chances

Don't worry if you get a word wrong the first time! Here's what happens:

  1. 1First try: Type what you think the word is and click "Submit Answer".
  2. 2If it's wrong: The box will turn orange and the computer will read the sentence with that word.
  3. 3Second try: Listen carefully and try again!
  4. 4Don't worry: Even if you need two tries, you're still learning!
Tip: Listen carefully to how the word sounds in the sentence. This will help you spell it correctly!

‚ú® Spelling Tips

Listen Carefully

  • Play the audio as many times as you need
  • Pay attention to each sound in the word
  • Think about words you already know that sound similar

Use the Letter Spaces

  • Count the dashes to see how many letters the word has
  • This helps you know if your spelling is the right length
  • If your word is too short or too long, try again

Common Patterns

Silent letters: Some letters you can't hear

knife
lamb
island

Double letters: Some sounds need two letters

happy
little
address

üèÜ Understanding Your Results

Your Score

At the end, you'll see:

  • Total Score: How many words you got right (out of 15)
  • First Try: Words you spelled correctly the first time
  • Second Try: Words you needed two attempts for
  • Time Taken: How long the quiz took you

Word-by-Word Results

You'll see each word with:

  • ‚úÖ Green: You got it right!
  • üü° Orange: You got it right on the second try
  • ‚ùå Red: You can practice this word more
Remember: Every quiz helps you learn! Don't worry about perfect scores - focus on learning new words.

üîß If Something Goes Wrong

Audio Not Working?

  • Check your speakers or headphones are plugged in
  • Make sure your device volume is turned up
  • Try refreshing the page (reload the website)
  • Ask your teacher for help if needed

Can't Type in the Boxes?

  • Click directly in the text box
  • Make sure you've submitted the previous answer first
  • Try refreshing the page if it's not working

Page Looks Wrong?

  • Try making your browser window bigger
  • Refresh the page (reload the website)
  • Ask your teacher to check your internet connection
